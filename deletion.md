# User Data Deletion Instructions

**Effective Date:** 2026-02-11

JaiBaan Juristic application is built for educational and management purposes. If you want to request the deletion of your personal data associated with this application, you can do so by following these steps:

1.  Send an email to us at **jaibaan.contact@gmail.com** with the subject "Data Deletion Request".
2.  Please include your registered email address or account name in the email body.
3.  We will process your request and delete your data within 30 days.
4.  You will receive a confirmation email once the deletion is complete.
